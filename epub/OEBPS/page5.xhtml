As he looks up he sees a girl,
empty bento in hand,
ready to throw it,
along with a crumpled soda can.
“Stop! Think, before that rubbish you fling,
don’t you know the kind of damage you’d bring?
You’ve learned, I’m sure, there’s proper places to put all your stuff.
It’s the same with litter. Put it in trash cans, they’re not used often enough!”
“Oh my,” said the girl, “I wasn’t thinking, I’m sorry.
From now on, I’ll remember your advice, don’t worry.”
“Mahalo! Remember, whatever you dump ends up in the ocean,
And for us who live there, it’s a real scary notion.”
