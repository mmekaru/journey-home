But wait, what’s this? The stream entrance is clogged.
Junk and gunk are everywhere, this water is really fogged.
On his way up he asks an opae,
who just so happened to be passing by,
“What’s with all this opala I see all around?”
“Brah, I dunno, but there’s a lot to be found!
I don’t really know where all this junk comes from,
but it sure makes the place look pretty much like a slum.”
